Request for Repairs

Know your rights and make a formal request to your landlord

Your Basic Rights

As a tenant in the UK, your landlord is legally responsible for keeping the property in good repair. This includes:

  • Plumbing and heating systems
  • Gas safety and electrical systems
  • Structural repairs (walls, roofs, windows)
  • Keeping common areas safe and clean

If repairs are needed, you have the right to ask your landlord in writing, formally requesting the repairs be done within a reasonable time.
